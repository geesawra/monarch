# Privacy Policy for Monarch

**Last updated: April 13, 2026**

Monarch is an open-source Bluesky client for Android.

## Data Collection

### Stored on your device
- **Bluesky session tokens**: Used to authenticate with Bluesky servers. Stored locally, never sent to us.
- **App preferences**: Your settings and preferences.

### Sent to third parties
- **Crash reports**: Anonymized crash data sent to Firebase Crashlytics to help fix bugs.
- **Analytics**: Basic usage analytics via Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns.
- **Push notifications**: If enabled, your device token is stored on our server to deliver notifications.

### Your Bluesky content
All your posts, likes, follows, and other Bluesky activity go directly to Bluesky's servers. We do not store or process your content.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell your data
- We don't show ads
- We don't track you across apps
- We don't store your password

## Third-party services

- **Bluesky**: Your use of Bluesky is governed by their [Privacy Policy](https://bsky.social/about/support/privacy-policy)
- **Firebase**: Crash and analytics data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Open source

Monarch is open source. You can review the code at [github.com/geesawra/monarch](https://github.com/geesawra/monarch).

## Child safety

Monarch's standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including how to report violations, are published in [Child Safety Standards](child-safety-standards.md).

## Contact

Questions or concerns? Open an issue on GitHub or reach out on Bluesky.
